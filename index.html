About Sharada Vidhya Mandir

Established in 2066, Sharada Vidhya Mandir is a premier SEE level secondary school dedicated to providing quality education for grades nursery to 10. We specialize in preparing students for the Secondary Education Examination (SEE) and guiding them towards successful higher education pathways.

Our school follows the national curriculum with additional focus on SEE preparation, ensuring students not only pass with flying colors but also develop the skills and knowledge needed for their future academic journey in +2 and beyond.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional SEE level education that prepares students for successful completion of their secondary education and smooth transition to higher studies.

Our Vision

To be the leading SEE level school that produces confident, competent, and well-prepared students ready for higher education challenges.

SEE Preparation

Comprehensive preparation for Secondary Education Examination with proven track record of success.

Career Guidance

Expert guidance for higher education choices and career path planning after SEE completion.

Character Development

Building strong moral values and leadership qualities in teenage students.

Holistic Growth

Beyond academics, we focus on sports, arts, and co-curricular activities for overall development.
